Add a team member

What we need

Adding a team member will give them access to Local Authority Payments (LAPs). Their access may be different depending on their service role. This can be one or more of the following:

  • Head of Finance
  • Head of Waste
  • Finance Officer
  • Waste Officer

You will need to tell us:

  • their local authority email address
  • What service role they Wil have on Local Authority Payments (LAPs)
  • if they will be an administrator for the organisation
