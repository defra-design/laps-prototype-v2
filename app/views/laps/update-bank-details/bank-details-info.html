Update your bank details

We need your local authority's latest bank details to make sure pEPR payments are sent to the correct account.

How it works

  • Submit your updated bank details securely using the online form
  • We will contact your local authority's authorised finance officer (e.g. Section 151 officer) to confirm the details
  • You will receive an email once your updated bank details are verified and approved
