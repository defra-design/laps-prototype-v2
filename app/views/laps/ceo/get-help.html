Contact us

You can use any of the following methods to contact us.
We aim to respond to your request within 5 working days but complex queries may take up to 20 days.

We will contact you by email if we:

  • need more information
  • think it is going to take us longer to respond to your request

Raise a customer service ticket

You can raise a customer service ticket to:

  • ask a question about your payments
  • make a complaint
  • get help with your account
